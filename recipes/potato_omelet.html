Potato Omelet

This is delicious and really quick to make.

Ingredients

  • Half a cup of olive oil
  • 200g of thinly sliced potatoes
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 large thinly sliced onion
  • 4 eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tomatoes - peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped
  • 2 chopped green onions

Steps:

  1. In a large frying pan or skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.

    Sprinkle potatoes lightly with salt and pepper. Cook until golden brown and crisp.

  2. Once the potatoes are golden, stir in the onions. Cook, stirring occasionally,

    until onions soften and begin to brown.

  3. Meanwhile, beat eggs together with salt and pepper. Pour eggs into pan and

    stir gently to combine. Reduce heat to low and cook until eggs begin to brown on the bottom.

  4. Loosen bottom of omelet with a spatula, invert a large plate over the pan, and

    carefully turn the omelet out onto it. Slide the omelet back into the pan with the

    uncooked side down. Cook until eggs are set. Garnish omelet with tomato and

    green onion and serve warm.
