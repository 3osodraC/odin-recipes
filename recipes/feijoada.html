Feijoada

Brazilian black bean stew.

Feijoada is a traditional brazilian stew. It's excellent served over rice.

This is translated from portuguese, so there might be some inaccuracies.

Ingredients:
  • 1kg of black beans
  • 100g of dried meat
  • 70g of pig's ear
  • 70g of pig's tail
  • 70g of pig's foot
  • 100g of pork rib
  • 50g of pork loin
  • 200g of sausages

    • Seasoning:

  • 2 chopped onions
  • 1 bunch of chopped green onions
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • Black pepper (any amount)
  • 40ml of liquor
  • Salt (if needed)
Steps:

  1. Soak the meat for 36 hours or more, renew the water periodically.

    If the room temperature is hot, put some ice on top.

  2. Cook them in this order: hard meat, and then the soft meat.
  3. When they're soft, throw the beans in and remove the meat.
  4. Season the beans.
Side dishes:

  • Cabbage, rice, oranges, pork chops, farofa, pumpkin kebab, bacon,

    pork rinds and seasoned broth.
